ABOUT

My name is Sastelvio Serafim Manuel, I am a University Teacher at the Catholic University of Mozambique and a Software Developer. I believe that for great results we should have equally great aspirations, however, I know the importance of keeping solid roots as support, so I focus and dedicate myself to what I believe to be essential to achieving my goals. Information Technology was a consequence of a breakdown in my life, but I am fascinated by the semantics and logic behind it. Recently, I had the opportunity to start developing Energy-related skills and knowledge so I accepted this challenge because I believe that in the current scenario of energy demand due to population increase, industrialization (among other factors) has as one of the main consequences of the increase of emissions of harmful gases to the planet year after year, it is expected that a sustainable future based on renewable energies will be supported by information technologies to optimize the production, storage, distribution and consumption of energy.
